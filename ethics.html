IT Ethics

Developer Ethics

Developers must design systems that are safe, secure, and respect user privacy. According to the ACM Code of Ethics, professionals should avoid harm and act in the public interest.

Data Science Ethics

Data scientists must ensure fairness, avoid bias in algorithms, and protect sensitive data. Transparency and accountability are essential when working with data.

Privacy & Security

Users’ personal data must be protected through strong security practices and responsible data handling.
